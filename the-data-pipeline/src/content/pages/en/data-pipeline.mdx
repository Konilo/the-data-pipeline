The purpose of **Data Professionals** is to collect data, process it to extract its value, and, most importantly, communicate the resulting information in an efficient way to end users.

Those operations are conducted throughout what is called the **Data Pipeline**. The Data Pipeline is made of conduits, processing plants, and storage facilities which organize the **Data Flow** â€“ the movement and transformation of data along the pipeline.
